Main datasets

D4_study_source_population

The unit of observation of this table is all the persons that have passed the exclusion criteria regarding the population excluding the insufficient runin.





D4_study_population

The unit of observation of this table is all the persons that have passed all the exclusion criteria. The table contains basic demographic variables and period inside the study.





D3_study_population

In this table, covariates are included(age, at-risk conditions) at the three times: study entry date and date vax 1. Persons included come from D4_study_population

* risk_factor may be equal to: cancer, diabetes, obesity, cardiovascular disease, chronic lung disease, chronic kidney disease, hiv, or sickle cell disease.





D4_study_population_cov

In this table, dates of vaccination (if any) are included, as well as covariates (age, at-risk conditions) at study entry date. Persons included come from D4_study_population





D3_vaccin_cohort

Unit of observation: persons in D3 study_population with date_vax1 non missing.

*risk_factor may be equal to: cancer, diabetes, obesity, cardiovascular disease, chronic lung disease, chronic kidney disease, hiv, or sickle cell disease.





D3_studyweeks

Unit of observation: persons in D3 study_population. Observations: one per week since date_vax_1 to study_exit_date (including partial weeks, if any).





D3_vaxweeks

Unit of observation: persons in D3 vaccin_cohort. Observations: one per week study_start_date to study_exit_date (including partial weeks, if any).





D3_vaxweeks_including_not_vaccinated

Unit of observation: persons in D3 study_population. Observations: one per week from date_vax_1 to study_exit_date (including partial weeks, if any), one week for not vaccinated. In addition with respect to D3_vaxweeks, this table contains birth cohort and if the person has any risk factors
